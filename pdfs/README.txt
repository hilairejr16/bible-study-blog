UPLOAD YOUR PDF LESSON FILES INTO THIS FOLDER
=============================================

How to name your files:
- Use lowercase letters and hyphens (no spaces)
- Examples:
    power-of-prayer.pdf
    armor-of-god.pdf
    walking-in-the-spirit.pdf
    fruits-of-the-spirit.pdf

After uploading a PDF here, add the lesson to lessons.json so it appears on the blog.
See README.md for step-by-step instructions.
